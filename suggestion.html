Academic References & Methodology

Primary Research Sources

  • World Health Organization (WHO). (2021). Obesity and overweight fact sheet. Geneva: WHO Press.
  • National Institutes of Health (NIH). (2020). Clinical Guidelines on the Identification, Evaluation, and Treatment of Overweight and Obesity in Adults.
  • Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). (2022). Body Mass Index: Considerations for Practitioners.
  • American College of Sports Medicine. (2021). Exercise Guidelines for Weight Management.

Methodology & Algorithms

BMI Calculation: Standard formula: weight (kg) / [height (m)]²

Classification: WHO International Classification (2000)

Time Estimation: Based on safe weight change rate of 0.5 kg/week (NIH recommendation)

Risk Assessment: Derived from epidemiological studies on BMI and health outcomes

Nutritional Guidelines Sources

  • U.S. Department of Agriculture Dietary Guidelines for Americans (2020-2025)
  • Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics Evidence Analysis Library
  • European Food Safety Authority Scientific Opinions
  • Indonesian Ministry of Health Dietary Guidelines
